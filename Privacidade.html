Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains, in clear language, how Vista do Lago Jungle Lodge collects, uses and protects your personal data.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Our Commitment

We process personal data under Brazil's LGPD (Law 13.709/2018), aligned with GDPR principles for international guests. Using the site, booking or contacting us implies agreement with this policy.

2. Who Controls Your Data

Controller: Vista do Lago Jungle Lodge, Rio Negro region, near Manaus, Amazonas, Brazil.

Contact: reservas@vistadolagojunglelodge.com.br / WhatsApp +55 92 98454-5630.

3. What Data We Collect

  • Booking data — name, e-mail, phone, nationality, document, payment and preferences (via Cloudbeds).
  • Contact data — form, e-mail, WhatsApp.
  • Chat assistant data — messages you send to the website chat about availability, rates and rooms.
  • Navigation data — IP, browser, pages visited and cookies.

4. Why We Use Your Data

  • Manage your reservation, check-in and stay.
  • Answer your questions.
  • Operate transfers, activities and packages.
  • Improve our site and services.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, guest registration and lodging safety rules in Brazil).
  • Send offers only with your consent.

5. Legal Bases

  • Performance of a contract (your reservation).
  • Legal obligation.
  • Legitimate interest (security and improvement).
  • Consent (marketing and non-essential cookies).

6. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with partners strictly necessary to serve you:

  • Cloudbeds — reservations / PMS.
  • Payment processors.
  • Cloudflare — hosting and security.
  • Communication tools (WhatsApp, e-mail).
  • Review platforms (Google / TripAdvisor widgets, with their own cookies).
  • Public authorities, when required by law.

All partners are bound to adequate security measures.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for language preference, security, analytics and embedded content (videos, review widgets). You can manage them in your browser; some features may not work properly without them.

8. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for the purposes above, to meet legal obligations (guest records, tax documents) or to defend legal claims. After that, it is deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

Under the LGPD and GDPR, you may request:

  • Confirmation and access to your data.
  • Correction of incomplete or outdated data.
  • Anonymization, blocking or deletion.
  • Data portability.
  • Information about sharing.
  • Withdrawal of consent and opt-out of marketing.

Requests to reservas@vistadolagojunglelodge.com.br, answered within legal deadlines.

10. Security

We use HTTPS, restricted access and trusted providers to protect your data. No system is 100% immune; in case of an incident, we notify you as required by law.

11. International Transfers

Providers such as Cloudbeds and Cloudflare may process data abroad, always with LGPD safeguards (contractual clauses and adequate protection).

12. Children

Our services are intended for adults. Minors' data is processed only within a family reservation and provided by the responsible adult.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes are published on this page, with the date shown at the top.
