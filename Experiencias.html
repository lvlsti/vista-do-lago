Amazon Experiences

Real Encounters with the Living Amazon

From pink river dolphins to indigenous villages and night wildlife — every stay is filled with authentic, guided experiences along the Rio Negro.

01
Wildlife

Pink River Dolphin Encounter

Get up close with the legendary pink dolphins of the Rio Negro in their natural habitat — a magical, respectful encounter with one of the Amazon's most iconic creatures.

Half / Full day Local guide
02
History

Ariaú Hotel Ruins

Explore the haunting skeleton of the once world-famous Ariaú Towers, a treetop hotel slowly reclaimed by the river and forest — a striking reminder of the Amazon's power.

Half / Full day Local guide
03
Tradition

Native Fishing on the Rio Negro

Try traditional Amazonian fishing alongside local guides, casting for piranhas and native species the way riverside communities have for generations.

Half / Full day Local guide
04
Rainforest

Virgin Rainforest Trail

Walk deep into untouched rainforest with an expert guide, discovering medicinal plants, towering trees, and the living sounds of the jungle.

Half / Full day Local guide
05
Culture

Kubewa Indigenous Village

Visit the Kubewa community to experience authentic indigenous culture — traditional dance, crafts, and ancestral knowledge shared by the people of the forest.

Half / Full day Local guide
06
Night

Night Wildlife Spotting

Head out after dark to spot caimans and nocturnal wildlife by lamplight, gliding silently across the river under a sky full of stars.

Half / Full day Local guide
07
Hands-on

Cassava Flour House

Discover how the Amazon's staple food is made, joining locals to roast and prepare manioc flour in a traditional cassava house.

Half / Full day Local guide

Good to Know

Important Activity Notes

Activities follow a daily base schedule, with timing set by the lodge according to weather, logistics and operation.

An activity may be changed or cancelled for safety, weather, guide availability or extraordinary situations.

No refund is given for an activity not enjoyed by the guest (change of interest, indisposition, personal delay, etc.).

Guests must be ready at the departure point at the informed time. If absent at departure, the activity leaves without them — considered a no-show, with no refund or rescheduling.

By taking part or booking, the guest automatically accepts these rules, ensuring an organized and fair experience for everyone.
