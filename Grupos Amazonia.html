Day by Day

A Taste of the Expedition

A sample flow of your days on the Rio Negro — adjusted by season and your group.

01Day

Arrival & River Transfer

Pickup in Manaus and a scenic boat journey along the Rio Negro to the lodge. Settle into your group apartment and enjoy a welcome dinner.

02Day

Rainforest Trails & Canoeing

Morning jungle trail with your local guide, afternoon canoe exploration of the flooded forest and quiet igarapés.

03Day

Community & Wildlife

Visit a riverside community to experience local culture, then a wildlife-spotting outing at golden hour.

04Day

Sunrise & Departure

Early sunrise activity on the river, breakfast, and return transfer to Manaus with the group. (4-night option only.)
