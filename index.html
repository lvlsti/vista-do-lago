Our Essence

Rooted in Nature, Guided by the Amazon

We believe the Amazon is not just a destination. It is a living experience shaped by nature, culture, and the rhythm of the Rio Negro.

At Vista do Lago Jungle Lodge, every stay is designed to connect guests with the forest, the river, and the local way of life. Our lodge combines comfort, authentic experiences, and respect for the environment, creating a peaceful gateway to discover the real Amazon.

What's Included in Your Stay

Our daily rate is a complete package. Whether you book directly with us or through our partners, your stay already includes all of this:

Included in every booking
  • Bungalow accommodation
  • Full board — breakfast, lunch and dinner
  • Transport Manaus ↔ boat meeting point
  • Boat transfer — meeting point ↔ Lodge
  • 2 guided activities per day

Available activities

You choose 2 per day from our program:

River dolphin interaction Caiman spotting Soap workshop Cassava flour house Rainforest trail Stargazing Sunset Piranha fishing Freshwater beach Amazonian sweets workshop (evening) Handicraft workshop Rio Negro Turtle Project visit Ariaú Hotel Ruins
