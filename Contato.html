Find Us on the Map

We are located on the banks of Lago do Acajatuba, about 70 km from Manaus along the Rio Negro.

By Speedboat (Direct)

Depart from the Manaus port area. About 1h30 by speedboat directly to the lodge pier. We can arrange transfers on request.

By Road + Motorboat

Drive along AM-070 to the meeting point (about 1h30 from Manaus), then 25 minutes by motorboat to the lodge. Parking available at the meeting point.
